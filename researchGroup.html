Open Positions

I am always on the lookout for excellent Ph.D. students and postdocs who are interested in theoretical research in the areas of machine learning, high-dimensional statistics, information processing, and networks. Applications that have recently motivated my work include neuroscience, power system monitoring, sequential decision making, and pandemic prediction and prevention. If you think there is a match between our interests, please send me an email.

I would be particularly thrilled to receive applications from people in underrepresented groups in machine learning, such as women and BIPOC candidates.
